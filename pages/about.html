The Story

About

RJ
Rohan Jasani — Designer

I design things that matter.

I'm Rohan — a designer based wherever the work takes me. I specialise in brand identity, product design, and design systems for ambitious companies that refuse to blend in.

Design, to me, is problem-solving with taste. Every pixel, every word, every interaction is a decision — and I believe getting those decisions right is what separates good work from work that endures.

Philosophy

01

Clarity over complexity

The best designs communicate instantly. I strip away everything that doesn't serve the message until only the essential remains.

02

Systems thinking

Great brands and products are built on strong foundations. I design with scalability in mind — so the work grows without breaking.

03

Honest craft

I care deeply about the details — kerning, spacing, weight, motion — because the people on the other end notice, even when they can't say why.

04

Collaboration first

The best work happens in partnership. I embed myself in your context, listen hard, then push the work to places you didn't expect.
